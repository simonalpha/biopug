What is programming?

  • Fundamentally, telling a computer what to do
  • In reality, have to figure out how to get the computer to do what you want
  • Ensuring the program actually does what you want it to do, consistently

Why programming?

  • Consistency in repetition
  • Understand the underlying processes in programs you use
  • Sharpen problem-solving and critical thinking
  • Be lazy!

Why Python?

  • Easy to learn
  • Easy to understand
  • Can be used to explore, or to write programs
  • Abstracts away a lot of the difficult stuff
  • A lot of code easily available
  • Currently very popular in science

How to think like a programmer

The Basics
  • How would I explain how to do this to a small child?
  • Be lazy! (Is there an easier way? Do I have to do this now?)
  • DON'T REPEAT YOURSELF (or anyone else, if you can avoid it)
  • Break it down into manageable chunks (abstract)
